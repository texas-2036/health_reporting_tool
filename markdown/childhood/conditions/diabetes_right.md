## Reducing the Risk of Developing Diabetes

* Drink fewer sugary drinks and eat more fruits and vegetables.
* Increase physical activity, with a goal of 60 minutes per day.

## Who has prediabetes?

* 1 in 5 aged 12-18 years^[1]
